Shipping Policy

We currently ship across India. All orders are processed within 1–2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation.

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Cost
Standard Shipping 5–7 business days ₹70
Express Shipping 2–3 business days ₹150
Free Shipping 5–7 business days Free on orders above ₹999
Please Note: Delivery timelines may vary for remote pin codes. You will receive a tracking link via email/SMS once your order has been dispatched.
  • Orders placed after 2 PM IST will be processed the next business day
  • All shipments are tamper-proof sealed for hygiene and safety
  • COD (Cash on Delivery) is available on select orders
  • We do not ship internationally at this time

Return Policy

We want you to love your Quint Beauty products. If you're not completely satisfied, we accept returns within 7 days of delivery, subject to the following conditions:

Eligible for Return

  • Product received is damaged or defective
  • Wrong product delivered
  • Product is unused, unopened, and in its original sealed packaging
  • Product received past its expiry date

Not Eligible for Return

  • Products that have been opened, used, or swatched
  • Items purchased during sale or promotional offers (unless defective)
  • Products without original packaging and tags
  • Return requests raised after 7 days of delivery
Hygiene Policy: Due to the nature of cosmetic products, we cannot accept returns on items that have been opened or used. This is to ensure the safety and well-being of all our customers.

How to Initiate a Return

Follow these simple steps to begin your return process:

1

Contact Us

2

Get Approval

Our team will review and approve your return within 24–48 hours

3

Ship It Back

Pack the product securely and ship it to the address provided

4

Get Refund

Refund initiated within 5–7 business days of receiving the product

Refund Policy

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email notification regarding the approval or rejection of your refund.

  • Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days
  • For prepaid orders, the refund will be credited to your bank account or UPI
  • For COD orders, refund will be processed via bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS)
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error
  • Partial refunds may be granted for items not in original condition
Exchange: If you'd like to exchange a product instead of a refund, let us know in your return request. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

Damaged or Missing Items

If your order arrives damaged or if items are missing, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with:

  • Your order number
  • Clear photographs of the damaged product and packaging
  • A brief description of the issue

We will arrange a free replacement or full refund for damaged/missing items — no questions asked.
